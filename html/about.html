ABOUT

NEW CLASSIC ARMS COMPANY

FORCE BEHIND THE FORCES

New Classic Arms (NCA) in its present form is a prodigy of an entrepreneurial family dealing in the Sporting Arms and Defense Weapons trade for different centuries. Its present work premises is situated in Industrial Estate, Kohat Road, Peshawar & Main G.T road, Azakhel Bala, Nowshera. It has been an engine for technological advancement in Sporting Arms and Defense Weapons production in the private sector in Pakistan.

New Classic Arms (NCA) is due to its adoption of State of the Art CNC technology coupled with in-house CAD/CAM facilities. To achieve this end, New Classic has invested to a great extent in Machinery, Equipment and in its Work Force. New Classic was one of the pioneers in introducing CNC machine technology in Pakistan.

New Classic has adopted the methodology of continuous improvement in its organization in every sphere of its activities. In New Classic, we give top priority to the quality so as to ensure customer satisfaction. Exquisite workmanship and waste elimination is given due care in the production of various products relating to Sporting Arms, Defense Weapons and Armaments.

New Classic moved into its present premises in early 2012s as a small industry. Today, by the grace of God it covers a large area. We humbly bow our heads to God Almighty for our success and thank our customers for the trust & confidence reposed in us and for their continued loyalty.
